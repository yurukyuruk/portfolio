Asli Yuruk

I'm a UX Designer and Web Developer with experience in creating custom websites and applications that blend functionality with design. I've worked on various projects worldwide, from developing websites to leading teams and contributing to rebuilding efforts. Additionally, I have an architecture background, having contributed to various architectural projects and technical drawings. This background gives me a unique perspective on design and structure, which I apply to my web development projects for clean, organized layouts.

I also have hands-on experience leading teams in non-tech environments, such as managing housekeeping operations at a hotel in New Zealand. There, I learned to plan, organize, and motivate teams to achieve their goals efficiently, and I bring these skills to every project I work on today.

Furthermore, I volunteered with an international team in Nepal, helping to rebuild the village of Laprak after an earthquake. This experience improved my teamwork and communication skills and showed me the power of collaboration.

Outside of the work, I enjoy hiking, exploring philosophy, learning languages, and indulging in food tasting.
