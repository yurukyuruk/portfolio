Asli Yuruk

I'm a Web Developer, and I create digital solutions that combine technical precision with thoughtful design. I've worked on various projects worldwide, from developing custom websites to leading teams and contributing to rebuilding efforts. Additionally, I have an architecture background, having contributed to various architectural projects and technical drawings. This background gives me a unique perspective on design and structure, which I apply to my web development projects for clean, organized layouts.

I've also gained leadership experience in some non-tech environments, such as supervising housekeeping operations at a hotel in New Zealand. There I learned how to plan, organize, and motivate a team to reach goals efficiently, and I carry these skills into every project. I also joined an international volunteer team in Nepal to help rebuild the village of Laprak after an earthquake. That experience strengthened my teamwork and communication skills and showed me the power of collaboration.

Outside of work, I enjoy challenges both physical and mental, from hiking trails to exploring philosophy or learning new languages. I also have a real passion for food tasting, which is my favorite way to explore the world.
